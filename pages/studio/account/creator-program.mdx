Hedra provides collaboration opportunities for content creators, filmmakers, and partners interested in shaping the future of content creation.

## Creator Program

### How to Apply

Interested creators can submit applications through Hedra's Tally form.

<Note>
Due to the high volume of submissions, it may take **1-2 weeks** to review your application.
</Note>

### Program Benefits

Approved creators gain access to:
