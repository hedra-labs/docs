---
title: "Forgot Password"
description: "How to reset your Hedra account password"
---

If you've forgotten your password and can't access your account, follow these steps to reset it.

## Reset Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Login Page">
    Navigate to [hedra.com/login](https://hedra.com/login)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Password Reset">
    Click on the "Forgot password" link and enter your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Look for the password reset email in your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the Link">
    Click the reset link in the email and create a new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**Check your spam folder!** The password reset email may end up in your spam or junk folder.
</Warning>

<Tip>
If you still don't receive the email after checking spam, wait a few minutes and try again, or contact support.
</Tip>
