## Is There a Hedra Mobile App?

Hedra does not currently have a dedicated mobile application.

## How to Use Hedra on Mobile

The platform is accessible through web browsers on desktop and mobile devices. You can use Hedra by:

1. Opening your mobile browser (Safari, Chrome, etc.)
2. Navigating to [hedra.com](https://hedra.com)
3. Logging in to your account

<Tip>
For the best mobile experience, use the latest version of your preferred browser and ensure you have a stable internet connection.
</Tip>

## Mobile Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Add to Home Screen">
    You can add Hedra to your home screen for quick access. In Safari, tap the share button and select "Add to Home Screen."
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Downloading Content on iPhone">
