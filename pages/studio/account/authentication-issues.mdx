Learn how to resolve authentication issues when using Hedra.

## "Authentication Required" Message

If you're seeing an "Authentication Required" message, it typically means you've been logged out.

### Common Causes

- System maintenance
- Prolonged inactivity
- Security protocol modifications
- Session expiration

## How to Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log Out">
    Log out of your Hedra account completely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log Back In">
    Log back into your account with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
This simple logout/login process resolves the issue in most cases. Periodic re-authentication serves to protect your account security.
</Info>

## Still Having Issues?

If logging out and back in doesn't resolve the problem:

1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Check if Hedra is experiencing any service issues
4. Contact [support](https://hedra.com/support) if the problem persists
