Learn how to permanently delete your Hedra account.

## How to Delete Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile">
    Navigate to [hedra.com/profile](https://hedra.com/profile)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete Account">
    Select **Delete your account** and confirm the action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**Account deletion is permanent.** Once deleted, you will need to create a new account to access Hedra. All your data, credits, and generated content will be lost.
</Warning>

## Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Cancel Subscription" icon="pause" href="/pages/studio/billing/cancel-subscription">
    Keep your account but stop billing
  </Card>
  <Card title="Download Content" icon="download" href="/pages/studio/content-creation/download-content">
    Save your generated content first
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Recovering a Deleted Account

<Warning>
**Deleted accounts cannot be recovered.** If you delete your account and want to use Hedra again, you'll need to create a brand new account.
</Warning>
