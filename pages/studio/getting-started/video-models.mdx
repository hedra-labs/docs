This guide helps you select the appropriate video generation model based on your creative needs and budget.

## Model Comparison

| Model | Cost | Max Duration | Best For |
|-------|------|--------------|----------|
| **Character-3** | 3-6 credits/sec | 5 min | Talking heads, lip sync, tutorials |
| **Character-3 (540p)** | 3 credits/sec | 5 min | Budget-friendly lip sync |
| **Veo 3** | 100 credits/sec | 8 sec | High-quality cinematic |
| **Veo 3 Fast** | 55 credits/sec | 8 sec | Faster cinematic |
| **MiniMax Hailou-02 Pro** | 16 credits/sec | - | Physics-heavy motion |
| **MiniMax Hailou-02** | 8 credits/sec | - | Budget physics |
| **Kling 2.1 Master** | 35 credits/sec | - | Premium 1080p |
| **Kling 2.1 Pro** | 15 credits/sec | - | Budget 720p/1080p |

## Model Details

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Hedra Character-3">
    **Cost:** 3-6 credits/sec (540p at 3 credits/sec)

    **Ideal for:** Talking heads, hosts, explainers, lip sync, podcasts, tutorials

    **Strengths:**
    - Native audio support (unique among these options)
    - Expressive performance
    - Precise lip-synchronization

    **Limitations:**
    - Not recommended for action sequences or physics-intensive scenes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Veo 3 / Veo 3 Fast">
    **Cost:** Veo 3: 100 credits/sec | Veo 3 Fast: 55 credits/sec

    **Ideal for:** High-quality cinematic footage

    **Strengths:**
    - Extremely high realism
    - Natural physics
    - Strong prompt adherence

    **Limitations:**
    - Limited to 8-second clips
    - Character consistency across multiple shots can be challenging
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="MiniMax Hailou-02 Models">
    **Cost:** Pro: 16 credits/sec | Standard: 8 credits/sec

    **Ideal for:** Physics-heavy motion (splashes, cloth, diving, dynamic stunts)

    **Strengths:**
    - Excellent physics simulation
    - Good for action sequences

    **Limitations:**
    - Standard version has lower detail than Pro
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Kling 2.1 Models">
    **Cost:** Master: 35 credits/sec | Pro: 15 credits/sec

    **Ideal for:** Polished, high-resolution output

    **Strengths:**
    - Master offers premium 1080p quality
    - Pro is cost-effective at 720p/1080p

    **Limitations:**
    - Higher cost than some alternatives
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
