Learn how to redeem promotional codes for your Hedra subscription.

## Steps to Redeem a Promo Code

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile">
    Navigate to your profile settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Billing">
    Select the **Billing** option from your account menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Subscription">
    Choose **Update subscription** to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Plan">
    Pick your desired subscription plan and select continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Code">
    Look for and click the **Add promotion code** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Enter your code and click **Apply**, then **Confirm**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Possible Results

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Invalid Code">
    The entered code is not recognized or valid in the system. Double-check your code for typos.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Valid Code, Wrong Plan">
    Your code works but doesn't apply to the subscription tier you selected. Try selecting a different plan that matches your promo code.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Valid Code, Correct Plan">
    The promotion successfully applies! You'll see the discount reflected in your total.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
Make sure both your promo code and subscription plan are compatible to ensure successful redemption.
</Tip>
