Learn how to modify your subscription plan or buy extra credits.

## Accessing Plan Options

Locate the **red icon that displays your credit total** in the bottom left-hand corner of your Hedra account. This icon provides access to both plan upgrade and credit purchase options.

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    Select the upgrade option to see available subscription tiers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare Options">
    Use the toggle switch to compare monthly versus annual billing options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Plan">
    Choose your desired plan and proceed to checkout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Subscription modifications take effect at **the start of your next billing cycle**. For expedited changes, contact the support team.
</Warning>

## Purchasing Credit Packs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Buy Credits">
    Select the credit purchase option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Pack Size">
    View available pack sizes and select your desired amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Purchase">
    Finalize your transaction through checkout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Credit packs are available immediately** - you won't have to wait for the new billing cycle, unlike plan changes.
</Info>

## Key Differences

| Type | When Available | Expiration |
|------|---------------|------------|
| Plan upgrades | Next billing cycle | Reset each cycle |
| Credit packs | Immediately | Never expire |
