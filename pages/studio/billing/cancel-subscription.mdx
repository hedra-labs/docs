Learn how to cancel your Hedra subscription.

## How to Cancel

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Profile">
        Navigate to your profile icon in the bottom left.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Go to Billing">
        Select **Billing**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Cancel">
        Click **Cancel subscription** under your membership details.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Profile">
        Tap the profile icon in the top right.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Go to Billing">
        Choose **Billing**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Cancel">
        Tap **Cancel subscription** under your paid membership.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What Happens After Cancellation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Billing stops immediately">
    You won't be charged again after you cancel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Your account remains active">
    Your profile persists even after cancellationâ€”this is different from deleting your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep your credits">
    You maintain access to subscription credits through your current billing period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reactivate anytime">
    You can reactivate your subscription via the billing section whenever you're ready.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
Canceling your subscription is different from [deleting your account](/pages/studio/account/delete-account). Your account and data remain intact after cancellation.
</Warning>
