Learn how to retrieve receipts and invoices for your Hedra purchases.

## Automatic Receipts

You receive email receipts for all payments, including:
- Subscription payments
- Credit pack purchases

## Retrieving Invoices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Billing Profile">
    Navigate to your profile in the lower left corner of the Hedra application and select **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Invoice">
    In the **Invoice History** section, locate the specific transaction you need. Click on it to trigger a download popup for your receipt or invoice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can access your complete payment history at any time through the Billing section. No need to search through emails!
</Tip>
