Learn about accepted payment methods for Hedra subscriptions.

## How to Pay

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Plans">
    Go to [hedra.com/plans](https://hedra.com/plans)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Plan">
    Select your preferred subscription plan
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Checkout">
    Complete checkout via our secure Stripe payment system
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Accepted Payment Methods

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Credit Cards" icon="credit-card">
    - Visa
    - Mastercard
    - American Express
  </Card>
  <Card title="Other Cards" icon="money-check">
    - Debit cards
    - Prepaid Visa gift cards
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Currently Not Supported

The following payment methods are **not accepted** at this time:

- UPI
- PayPal
- Cryptocurrency

<Note>
There is no timeline for adding these payment options. During checkout, all viable payment options will be presented to you.
</Note>
