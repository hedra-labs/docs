---
title: "Voice Cloning"
description: "How to create and manage your own voice clone in Hedra"
---

Learn how to create and manage your own voice clone for use in videos.

## Creating a Voice Clone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Audio Panel">
    Navigate to the Audio panel in the interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Voice Selection">
    Select **Choose Voice** at the base of the text box.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Voice">
    Click **Create Voice** in the upper right section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record Your Voice">
    Read the provided script, either by:
    - Uploading a file with the recording being read
    - Recording directly from within the browser
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Your Voice">
    Select **Submit** and assign a name to your voice clone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Your voice clone is only ever available to you and won't be shared with other users.
</Info>

## Removing a Voice Clone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Voice Settings">
    Open the video generator and access the Audio Script section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Voice Selection">
    Select **Generate speech** and then **Choose voice** in the upper left area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View All Voices">
    Navigate to **View all voices** in the dropdown's lower section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete the Voice">
    Locate the voice you wish to remove and click the trash icon beside it, then confirm the deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Deleting a voice clone is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Warning>
