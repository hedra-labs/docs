---
title: "Downloading Generated Content"
description: "How to download your videos, images, and audio from Hedra"
---

Learn how to access and download your generated content.

## Steps to Download

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Library">
    Visit [hedra.com/app/library](https://hedra.com/app/library) or select the **Library** icon in the left sidebar on desktop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an Asset">
    Click on the specific asset you want to download.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    Click the middle downward pointing arrow (download icon) to download the content to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading on iPhone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Download">
    Tap the download button on your content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Downloaded File">
    Click on the bottom bar to access your downloaded file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Your Library retains all generated content, so you can always go back to download previous generations.
</Tip>
