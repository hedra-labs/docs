Learn how to create audio using Hedra's text-to-speech capabilities.

## Steps to Create Audio

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Audio Tool">
    Select the **Audio** icon from the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Script">
    Type your voiceover content in the text box.

    <Note>
    Character limits: **5,000 characters** for premium subscriptions, **200 characters** for free accounts.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Voice">
    Pick from available voices or upload a cloned voice. The platform supports multiple text-to-speech models:
    - ElevenLabs
    - Eleven V2
    - MiniMax

    You can filter voices by masculine or feminine preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Audio">
    Click **Generate** to create the TTS output.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Your Audio">
    Access your finished audio in the **History** section on the right sidebar where you can:
    - Preview
    - Regenerate
    - Download
    - Add to video
    - Delete
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customization Options

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Language Selection">
    Click the globe button (set to "Auto" by default) to specify which language should be spoken.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Speech Adjustment">
    Access the settings icon to modify speed and stability sliders for fine-tuning the generated speech quality.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Supported Audio Formats

When uploading your own audio instead of generating:

| Format | Supported |
|--------|-----------|
| .wav | Yes |
| .mp3 | Yes |

<Warning>
Maximum file size for audio uploads is **10MB**.
</Warning>

## Audio Length Limits

| Plan | Maximum Length |
|------|----------------|
| Free | 20 seconds |
| Premium | 5 minutes |
