---
title: "Download Content"
description: "How to download your generated videos, images, and audio from Hedra."
---

Once content is generated successfully, it can be accessed in your **Library** <Icon icon="folder" />

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Library">
    Visit [hedra.com/app/library](https://www.hedra.com/app/library) or click the folder icon on the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an asset">
    Hover over an asset to access the <Icon icon="ellipsis" /> options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    Click the middle downward-pointing arrow to download the content to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading on iPhone

On iPhone, after clicking download, tap the download bar at the bottom of the screen to save the file to your device.
