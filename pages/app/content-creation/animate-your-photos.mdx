## Using Generative AI Video

Bringing your photos to life has never been easier. The simpliest way to do this:

1. From the home page, click the **\+ References** button in the Agent prompt box
2. **Select** **Upload**
3. Find and **select the image** you want to animate
4. **Describe what you want** to happen in the video using the prompt box
5. Click the **Arrow** <Icon icon="arrow-up" />
6. The Agent will present a suggestion. You can **Deny** or **Approve** it
7. Once finished, click the <Icon icon="ellipsis" /> on your video to **Download** or **Share**
