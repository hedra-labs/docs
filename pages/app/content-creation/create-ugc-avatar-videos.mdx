This guide walks you through the full process: image audio video.

---

## [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#before-you-start)

**Before You Start**You will need the following:

- **Image** (uploaded or generated in Hedra)
- **Audio** (uploaded, recorded, or AI-generated)
- Access to video generation inside Hedra Studio

---

## [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#create-an-avatar-image)

**Create an Avatar Image**See: [**Create Images**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-image)

> Tip: Front-facing portraits produce the most natural speaking results.

---

## [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#create-the-avatar-voice)

**Create the Avatar Voice**You can add audio in three ways:

- **Upload** an existing audio file
- **Record** your voice directly in Hedra
- **Generate** AI voice using supported voice models

See: [**Create Voice**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-audio)

> Tip: Clear audio improves lip-sync accuracy.

---

## [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#select-an-avatar-video-model)

**Select an Avatar Video Model**

1. Go to the [**Avatar**](https://www.hedra.com/app/video) section
2. Choose an **avatar video model** (_Hedra Omnia, Avatar, Kling_) from the pop-up menu below the prompt box
3. Select **aspect ratio, resolution and batch size**
4. Confirm image and audio are both attached loaded

---

## [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#generate-the-video)

**Generate the Video**

1. In the prompt box, **describe your video**
2. Click  to **Generate**
3. Wait for processing to complete
4. Preview the result
5. On the video, select  to:\
   **View Details**, **Share**, **Download** or **Delete** if needed

---

## [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#best-practices)

**Best Practices**

- Use high-resolution images
- Avoid extreme angles or heavy shadows
- Keep audio clean and evenly paced
- Start with short clips when testing

---

## [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#troubleshooting)

**Troubleshooting**

### [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#the-lips-don%E2%80%99t-match-the-audio)

**The lips don’t match the audio**

- Check for background noise
- Ensure the audio file is clear and not distorted

### [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#the-face-looks-unnatural)

**The face looks unnatural**

- Use a well-lit, front-facing image
- Avoid heavy filters or stylization

### [**​**](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-avatar-video#the-video-failed-to-generate)

**The video failed to generate**

- Confirm both image and audio are attached
- Try regenerating
