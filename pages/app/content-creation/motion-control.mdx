---
title: "Using Motion Control"
description: "How to use motion control to animate videos with reference video motion."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Motion Control">
    Go to [Motion Control](https://www.hedra.com/app/video).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a start frame">
    Click **Add Start Frame** and select from: **Generate**, **Upload**, **Take Photo**, or **Library**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a reference video">
    Click **Add Video** and select from: **Upload**, **Record**, or **Assets**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select match orientation">
    Choose your **Match Orientation**:

    - **Video** — Orientation matches reference video; best for complex motion
    - **Character** — Orientation matches reference image; best for camera moves
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your prompt">
    In the prompt box, describe your video. Press Tab to expand. Use Ctrl+K for custom prompt enhancement instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select model and settings">
    Select **Kling 2.6 Motion Control Pro** in the model dropdown menu. Check your **Video Settings** — you can change the batch size (1–8) and pick your aspect ratio (16:9, 1:1, 9:16).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    - Click the **Magic Wand** to enhance your prompt
    - Click the **Up Arrow** to generate your video
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    - Select the ellipsis on your video to: **View Details**, **Share**, **Download**, or **Delete**
    - Click the **Turntable** icon on your video to remix it
  </Step>
</Steps>
