---
title: "Upscale Videos"
description: "How to upscale your videos to higher resolutions in Hedra."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Video page">
    Go to the [Video Page](https://www.hedra.com/app/video).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Upscale">
    At the top, click **All** and select **Upscale** from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a video">
    Click **Add Video**. You can pick from: **Upload Video**, **Record Video**, or **Assets**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select resolution">
    Choose the resolution: **1080p**, **2K**, or **4K**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upscale">
    Click the **Up Arrow** to upscale.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    - Select the ellipsis on your video to: **View Details**, **Share**, **Download**, or **Delete**
    - Click the **Turntable** icon on your video to remix it
  </Step>
</Steps>
