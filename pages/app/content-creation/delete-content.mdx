---
title: "Delete Content"
description: "How to permanently delete generated videos, images, or audio from your library."
---

If you'd like to permanently delete a generated asset, you can do so directly from your library.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Library">
    Navigate to your [Library](https://www.hedra.com/app/library) and find the asset you want to delete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the asset">
    Click the image, video, or audio file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete">
    Click the trash can icon to delete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deletion is permanent and files cannot be recovered. Bulk delete is not yet available but is on the roadmap.
</Warning>
