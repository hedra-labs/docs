---
title: "Speaker Selection"
description: "How to define which character should be speaking in a multi-character image."
---

If you have a starting image with multiple characters, Hedra has a tool that lets you select which character will be talking.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload or create a starting image">
    Upload or create the starting image with multiple characters.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the speaker">
    Click on the uploaded image and click the **Select Speaker** button that shows up in the bar in the middle of the screen when you hover over the image.
