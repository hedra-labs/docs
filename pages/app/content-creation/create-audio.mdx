## Additional Options

### Speech control tags

Add more control to your speech with tags in the script. Click on **Control**, then pick from **Emotional Tone**, **Reaction**, **Volume & Energy**, or **Pacing & Rhythm**. You can also type "/" in the editor to search for tags.
