---
title: "Using Live-Avatar"
---

1. Open the [**Live-Avatar**](https://www.hedra.com/app/avatar) app.
2. In the **[prompt box]**, describe your characters persona.
3. Select a **Realtime Model**:

   **OpenAI, ElevenLabs, Gemini** 
4. On the bottom, select an existing character or click **Customize**.
5. Drag and **drop an image** or **click to upload** one. You can also select **Capture Image **from a camera or **Create Image**.
6. If you're creating an image, select an image generator from the dropdown. In the **[prompt box]**, ask for a headshot of your character.
7. Click the **Up Arrow** to generate.
8. To select it, click **Use Image**.
9. Once ready, click the **Up Arrow** to connect with your avatar in a live chat.

   (Requires a microphone)
