---
title: "Use Live-Avatar"
---

Use Live Avatar to create and interact with a real-time AI character using voice.

## Open Live Avatar

Navigate to the [**Live Avatar**](https://www.hedra.com/app/avatar) app inside Hedra.

---

## Define Your Character

In the **Prompt** field, describe your character’s persona.

Include details like:

- Personality
- Tone
- Background
- Speaking style

---

## Choose a Realtime Model

Select one of the supported realtime models:

- OpenAI
- ElevenLabs
- Gemini

This model will power your avatar’s responses.

---

## Select or Create a Character Image

At the bottom of the screen:

- Select an existing character\
  **or**
- Click **Customize** to create a new one

You can:

- Drag and drop an image
- Click to upload
- Select **Capture Image** from your camera
- Click **Create Image** to generate one

---

## If Creating an Image

1. Choose an image generator from the dropdown
2. In the prompt field, request a headshot of your character
   - Example: _Professional studio headshot of a confident woman in her 30s_
3. Click the <Icon icon="arrow-up-long" /> to **generate**
4. Click **Use Image** to apply it

---

## Start the Live Session

Once your image and model are selected:

Click the <Icon icon="arrow-up" /> to connect with your avatar in live chat.

> A microphone is required for live interaction.
