## Before You Start

You will need the following:

- <Icon icon="image" /> **Image** (uploaded or generated in Hedra)
- <Icon icon="waveform-lines" /> **Audio** (uploaded, recorded, or AI-generated)
- Access to video generation inside Hedra Studio

---

## Create an Avatar Image

See: [Create Images](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-image)

> Tip: Front-facing portraits produce the most natural speaking results.

---

## Create the Avatar Voice

You can add audio in three ways:

- **Upload** an existing audio file
- **Record** your voice directly in Hedra
- **Generate** AI voice using supported voice models

See: [Create Voice](https://www.hedra.com/docs/pages/app/content-creation/create-audio)

> Tip: Clear audio improves lip-sync accuracy.

---

## Select an Avatar Video Model

1. Go to the [**Avatar**](https://www.hedra.com/app/video) section
2. Choose an **avatar video model** (_Hedra Omnia, Avatar, Kling_) from the pop-up menu below the prompt box
3. Select **aspect ratio, resolution and batch size**
3. Confirm image and audio are both attached loaded

---

## Generate the Video

1. In the prompt box, **describe your video**
1. Click <Icon icon="circle-arrow-up" /> to **Generate**
2. Wait for processing to complete
3. Preview the result
4. On the video, select <Icon icon="ellipsis" /> to:\
   **View Details**, **Share**, **Download** or **Delete** if needed

---

## Best Practices

- Use high-resolution images
- Avoid extreme angles or heavy shadows
- Keep audio clean and evenly paced
- Start with short clips when testing
