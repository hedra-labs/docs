## Swapping Faces

To swap faces on Hedra, you can access the tool directly here: [Templates | Hedra](https://www.hedra.com/app/templates?template=face-swap)

<Info>
  You need two images. The person being replaced and a picture of yourself, or the person being added.
</Info>

**Upload the face you want to insert:**\
This is the person/face that is getting replaced.

**Photo with face to replace:**\
This is a picture of you or the person you want to appear in the new image.

After inserting the images, select your aspect ratio (**9:16, 16:9**)

Click **Generate**
