---
title: "Voice Cloning"
description: "How to create and delete your voice clone in Hedra Studio."
---

If you're looking to use your own voice in Hedra Studio, you can use the voice cloning feature.

## Creating a Voice Clone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Audio panel">
    Go to the **Audio** panel in Studio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open voice selection">
    Click the **voice selection **dropdown at the top of the text box.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new voice">
    Select **Create Voice** in the upper right-hand of the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record or upload">
    Read the provided script, either by uploading a file with the recording or by recording directly from within the browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit Voice** and give it a name.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once you've done this, you'll be able to select that same voice from the **Choose Voice** setting.

<Note>
  Your voice clone is only ever available to you and won't be shared.
</Note>

## Deleting a Voice Clone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the video generator">
    Open the video generator and click **Audio Script**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to voices">
    Select **Generate speech**, then select **Choose voice** in the upper left corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View all voices">
    Click **View all voices** towards the bottom of the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete">
    Click the trash can <Icon icon="trash-can" /> icon next to the voice you'd like to delete and confirm **Yes**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
