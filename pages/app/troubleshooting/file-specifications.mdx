## Supported formats and limits

| Specification | Details |
|---------------|---------|
| **Audio file types** | `.wav` and `.mp3` |
| **Maximum file size** | 10 MB (applies to all file types, including audio and images) |
| **Max audio length (free plan)** | 20 seconds |
| **Max audio/video length (paid plans)** | Up to 10 minutes (model-dependent) |

## Audio length by model

| Model | Max Length |
|-------|-----------|
| Hedra Avatar | 10 minutes |
| Character-3 (legacy) | 5 minutes |
| Fabric | 5 minutes |
| Avatar V2 Pro | 5 minutes |
| OmniHuman | 5 minutes |
| Hedra Omnia | 8 seconds |

<Note>
  Hedra Omnia offers camera control, full body motion, and more natural movement but is currently capped at 8 seconds due to demand and compute availability.
</Note>
