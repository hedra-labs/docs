---
title: "Accessing Your Library"
description: "How to find and manage your generated assets in the Hedra Library."
---

To access your assets in the [Library](https://www.hedra.com/app/library), select the folder <Icon icon="folder" /> on the left sidebar from any page on Hedra.

Assets are organzied by: **All**, **Video**, **Image**, **Audio**, **Elements**, and **Favorites**.
