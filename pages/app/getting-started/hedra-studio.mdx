With Hedra Studio, you don't have to jump between tools to get the results you want. It combines the best image, audio, and video models like **Kling**, **Minimax**, and **Veo2** to help you create content fast and easily, all in one platform.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Image">
    Upload your own image or generate one with AI-powered tools like **Flux Kontext** or **Imagen4**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Audio">
    Create or upload audio using tools like **ElevenLabs** or your own recordings, then sync it to your visuals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Video">
    Use **Kling**, **Minimax**, or **Veo2** to produce stunning videos.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Tip>
