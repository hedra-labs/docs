## How Hedra Studio Works

Hedra Studio follows a simple creative flow:

### 1. Start with an Image <Icon icon="image" />

Upload your own image or generate one using Hedra’s built-in image models.

---

### 2. Add or Generate Audio <Icon icon="waveform-lines" />

Upload audio, record voice, or generate AI voice using supported voice models.

---

### 3. Generate Video <Icon icon="camera-movie" />

Select a video model and generate your final output.

---

## Core Components

### Image Generation

Create visuals from text prompts or edit uploaded images.

### Audio Generation

Generate or upload voice and sound for syncing with video.

### Video Models

Choose from available video generation models depending on your desired style and motion.

## Common Questions

### Do I need to start with an image?

Yes. Video generation begins with a visual input.

### Can I upload my own assets?

Yes. You can upload both image and audio files.

### Can I regenerate outputs?

Yes. You can adjust prompts or settings and generate again.
