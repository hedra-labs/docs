Hedra is a creative media platform that uses artificial intelligence to generate high-quality video, images, and audio. It's designed to be intuitive yet powerful for content creators, marketers, educators, artists, and developers alike.

## What you can create

- **Video** — Create cinematic clips, 10 minute avatar videos, and motion transfer with models like Hedra Omnia, Veo 3.1, Kling 3, Seedance and MiniMax
- **Images** — Generate unique visuals with AI models like Nano Banana Pro, Seedream 4.5, and Flux.2 Max
- **Audio** — Convert text to expressive speech using over 4000 voices including ElevenLabs, MiniMax, or your own cloned voice

## Get started

Sign up or log in at [hedra.com](https://hedra.com), then jump into one of the tools below.
