Hedra is a creative media platform that uses artificial intelligence to generate high-quality video, images, and audio. It's designed to be intuitive yet powerful for content creators, marketers, educators, artists, and developers alike.

## What you can create

- **Video** — Create avatar videos, cinematic clips, and dynamic scenes with models like Omnia, Veo, Kling, and MiniMax
- **Images** — Generate unique visuals with AI models like Flux Kontext, Seedream, and GPT Image
- **Audio** — Convert text to expressive speech using ElevenLabs, MiniMax, or your own cloned voice
- **Live Avatar** — Have real-time conversations with AI characters

Sign up or log in at [hedra.com](https://hedra.com), then jump into one of the tools below.
