---
title: "Receipts and Invoices"
description: "How to get a receipt or invoice for your Hedra payments."
---

For any payments, including subscriptions and credit packs, you should receive an email receipt. You can also get a copy at any time directly in your Hedra account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Click your profile in the lower left corner of the Hedra app and select the **Billing** link.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your invoice">
    Scroll to the **Invoice History** section and click the invoice you'd like to download. You'll be presented with a pop-up to download the receipt.
