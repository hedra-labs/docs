---
title: "Create an Element"
description: "How to create reusable elements like characters, environments, styles, and outfits."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Elements page">
    On the [image page](https://www.hedra.com/app/image), click **Elements** at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to My Elements">
    Under Elements, click **My Elements**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new element">
    Click **Create Element** below My Elements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload reference images">
    Drag and drop your images or click to upload (up to 10 reference images).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure and save">
    - Give it a **name**
    - Select the type: **Character**, **Environment**, **Style**, or **Outfit**
    - Add an optional description
    - Click **Save**
  </Step>
</Steps>
