---
title: "Generate an Image"
description: "How to generate AI images in Hedra Studio for use in videos and other content."
---

On Hedra, transform your ideas into images, ready for use in the Omnia video model and others.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Image tool">
    Click the **[+]** on the left sidebar, then select [Image](https://www.hedra.com/app/image) from the dropdown.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a model">
    Select a model from the dropdown (e.g., Nano Banana Pro, Seedream 4.5, GPT Image 1.5) located in the prompt box. Note the generation cost and description of what each model is best at.

    ![Choose model](https://assets.usepylon.com/bf1d331d-9547-4df2-941c-3ea3b66575f8%2F1770611432761-image.png?Expires=253370764800&Signature=C8aQa~vj1XtDNNYBTJFm0rlhWQzO7X46OzOGU7Xh6mg9BUBPVrTKg9IT2W4UhO~SzVF~pmx39EdeZYvWQA-4WsQwny085pNqnqeWVMc4asWZ4nt9IQrCLliZRSuiD9gMxcQ5RhCG8w4SxNCA16vPy7r7QfAuLeFa-mNT6D69hd7B9Yffd6OHG4Rr1Yv8dxF-DpBSpqdYyI15S3rkzwGNDKw4kK54kKkeBByG7I0R0FQ3wNQLP7GTnxHUq82yGuDKsOFvyiJFs5WCZjO56eooEN-jhAMufoKS957-wLfgy-H-LBfHlsGvroqJl4bapUtLMoNRnAE1TFpl08umeD45rg__&Key-Pair-Id=K3NV4LZ47N8M46)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure settings">
    Select your aspect ratio, resolution, and number of generations.

    <Note>
      Available options can vary by image model. To see what options are available, select one of the models on the list.
    </Note>

    ![Image settings](https://assets.usepylon.com/bf1d331d-9547-4df2-941c-3ea3b66575f8%2F1770611681197-image.png?Expires=253370764800&Signature=WT2bzLEMB5ezFR~~cajxmeXNlkuH9yOwu4lFK9buE8~fvgk53h6-gkrd8uxDGUpy2Q7tc828uhoEC5VC9m5ul7xz6fQvWVEdBztPV9zay3dq4g6tGEZAS3DZWWIpOVcdnZmBLEStPtqCItzsm7hOVMNWxvLwTqyUkvkIX08KT892~qzEDze088vn-yPEX3Z5Srog3V6iNpFNsD9m--VsZWngQGQbuCHr86Ec-Ch5iM--bW48A5QK4X~JD6aT3QUlD~h9hK-dIwzMIhG59oR8eGYXsDEsHlKGaUgz3F31CwNKdppmf0T1PU0cS3yph-8~soESy1XDcLKxQAbHoq-soQ__&Key-Pair-Id=K3NV4LZ47N8M46)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add reference images (optional)">
    Upload reference images or use prior assets as inspiration by clicking **+ Add Image to Edit or Incorporate**. You can choose from **Library**, **Elements**, **Upload**, or **Capture** in the dropdown.

    ![Add reference](https://assets.usepylon.com/bf1d331d-9547-4df2-941c-3ea3b66575f8%2F1770612408978-image.png?Expires=253370764800&Signature=ZDgg5kFz8q6UzOTQksc9nJ7D4os-0bL4oCjr5ZZ4zRych9KFUZP1eSp5SFAgCtQK-Js9oNxWzZNV9KL4yhKZzgyJk-XGmMtpK996fvZxTpPnQQbXsE3u1R49GPq~0Df5rbbB0klikdzFHGoo6Ojruzed1PZ0WBtgkXJP6epMHqeMaK1jXaYcMIpSCEYIp6pW9UAcSuG4I1OKjbxlQgALMxcZNnyyD-xuytRHqaxCoT0juorIYBhDzvpmjP6rr5KBsN7Ym9FA55TZWNvhXx~qVWemYpO9SUInRYPQUzAOI95GA2KRci214C-Db-kg872ZZwBp4hEHr97xF9qPihZJ7A__&Key-Pair-Id=K3NV4LZ47N8M46)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate** and the resulting generation(s) will show up in the space above the prompt bar.

    ![Generated image](https://assets.usepylon.com/bf1d331d-9547-4df2-941c-3ea3b66575f8%2F1761769910250-Screenshot2025-10-29at3.31.45PM.png?Expires=253370764800&Signature=Co2mIruFDDommybYIxjKE81W3ubWhWksnQaLiqWkLgipKIQj29P-mTBk5W3y0~~QTYhspk1zolSUHyqHQw~3qGMx8o3qVhVGfdMXo8Y~iz0qLWjSMqh3CmmclBd5AIKCUvmachzpafFiOXLYQ6vPo34XITneCEu0ALjAkhlofit0sqrKpgDpj0sFB5nlPf~~~j27yrWHUxqYiIELHPKsmTlFSidUDCoGOLzvmu7mKNiyrrllPqIlYVPYo1JCAr232mMP94STlE3ziP7o0bKnH-5St9vJ8ZYHhsehtfXDxlB0aSiME81r2oKpGOpjLbZP8BtlVAnrEEtDMp8c-HNaLg__&Key-Pair-Id=K3NV4LZ47N8M46)

    When hovering over an image you'll see multiple options along the bottom toolbar: (1) Create Video, (2) Remix, (3) Regenerate. In the upper-right corner you'll see options to (4) Download or (5) Full Screen.
